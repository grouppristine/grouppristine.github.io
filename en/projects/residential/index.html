Residential

Homes that hold a family's story

Overview

The residential sector, along with the commercial and retail sectors, has always been at the core of operations at Pristine. The group has ventured into all possible segments of the residential market since its inception and continues to diversify its portfolio.

Luxury Bungalows, Gated Villa Complexes and Affordable Housing currently form the group's residential portfolio. The group has successfully delivered projects in neighbourhoods such as New Friends Colony, Greater Kailash, South Extension, Kailash Colony, Chirag Enclave, Kalkaji, Malviya Nagar and Chattarpur, to name a few.

Luxury Farmhouses — a market the company has pioneered and is considered a market-mover in since its inception — are an extension of its residential portfolio.

Affordable Housing

Post the Covid-19 pandemic, the group further expanded its portfolio to include affordable flats and builder floors in Faridabad, where it is already prominently established across generations. Today, the company boasts a sizable inventory in this fast-moving segment.
