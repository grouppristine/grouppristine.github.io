Retail

Shopping, food and entertainment under one roof

Overview

Taking advantage of the economic boom and a sudden surge in demand for organised retail hubs in North India — standing true to its claim of dynamism — the Pristine Group successfully developed and delivered two landmark shopping malls in Faridabad and Khanna, Punjab.

Where We've Built

  • Faridabad, Haryana
  • Khanna, Punjab

Mall & Multiplex at Faridabad

Pristine Mall Faridabad is located in Sector-31 and surrounded by a great catchment, with over one million families residing within a 2 km radius. It is strategically located close to schools and colleges, open on all sides with three-side road connectivity.

Towering across 7 floors and covering 80,000 sq. ft. super area, the mall is fully air-conditioned and equipped with escalators, elevators and full power backup, along with a 600+ car park and a 3-screen multiplex.

It offers a hyper shopping store like Vishal Mega Mart mixed with great entertainment at our in-house Pristine Cinemas and kids' play zone.

Mall & Multiplex at Khanna, Punjab

Pristine City Centre Khanna, with great design aesthetics, offers value shopping, food and entertainment all under one roof.

Located a 40-minute drive from Ludhiana and a 60-minute drive from Chandigarh, the mall sits right on National Highway-1, which witnesses more than 1 million non-commercial vehicles plying.

A sanctioned and approved project with over 2,15,000 sq. ft. covered area, it is fully equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and ample parking for over 250 cars. The mall is fully air-conditioned with escalators, elevators, full power backup and a 2-screen multiplex.
