About

A legacy built on trust

Real Estate pioneers Late Shri Surender Kumar Gupta and Shri Rajendra Kumar Gupta, under the guidance of their father and the company's north star, Late Shri Lala Ram Bhaj Gupta, laid the foundation of Pristine Group in 1973. Today, the future generations of the Gupta Family are at the helm — scaling unprecedented heights and redefining real estate as we know it.

As one of the foundational builders of the Delhi-NCR region, the group has successfully developed a bouquet of real estate products across residential, commercial, retail, warehousing, industrial and luxury farmhouses, with roots and operations expanding into Punjab and Haryana.
